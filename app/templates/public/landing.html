Frequently asked questions

Is ScholarSync free for students?

Yes. Students browse, apply, and track applications at no cost. Scholarships are funded entirely by donors.

How does the match score work?

Your profile (GPA, major) is checked against each scholarship's eligibility criteria. The score shows the percentage of verifiable requirements you meet, so you can prioritize where to spend your time.

What happens after I'm selected?

You'll be notified in-app, accept the award with your payment details, and the donor disburses the funds with a documented payment trail you can both verify.

Can organizations run multiple scholarships?

Yes. Donors can run any number of scholarships, each with its own criteria, deadline, award slots, and review pipeline.
